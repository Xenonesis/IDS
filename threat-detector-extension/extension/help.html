Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about the extension

How does the AI threat detection work? ▼

Our AI threat detection uses advanced machine learning algorithms combined with multiple threat intelligence APIs including Google Safe Browsing, VirusTotal, PhishTank, and URLScan.io.

The system analyzes website content, URLs, scripts, and behavioral patterns in real-time to identify potential threats such as phishing attempts, malware distribution, suspicious redirects, and malicious downloads.

The AI continuously learns from new threat patterns and updates its detection capabilities to stay ahead of emerging security risks.

Does the extension slow down my browsing? ▼

No, the extension is designed for optimal performance with minimal impact on browsing speed. We use several optimization techniques:

• Intelligent Caching: Scan results are cached for 5 minutes to avoid repeated API calls

• Asynchronous Processing: All security checks run in the background without blocking page loads

• Lightweight Design: The extension uses minimal memory and CPU resources

• Smart Scanning: Only scans when necessary (page loads, content changes) rather than continuously

What types of threats can the extension detect? ▼

The extension can detect and protect against a wide range of online threats:

• Phishing Sites: Fake websites designed to steal credentials or personal information

• Malware Distribution: Sites hosting or distributing malicious software

• Suspicious Downloads: Potentially harmful files and executables

• Malicious Redirects: Unwanted redirects to dangerous websites

• Popup Attacks: Aggressive popups and overlay attacks

• Cryptocurrency Scams: Fake crypto exchanges and investment schemes

• Social Engineering: Sites using deceptive tactics to manipulate users

How do I configure the extension settings? ▼

To configure the extension settings:

1. Click the AI Threat Detector icon in your browser toolbar

2. In the popup, scroll down to the "Protection Settings" section

3. Toggle the switches for different protection features:

• Real-Time Scanning: Enable/disable automatic website scanning

• Popup Blocking: Control popup and overlay blocking

• Content Filtering: Manage ad and content blocking

• Threat Notifications: Configure alert preferences

4. Click "Save Settings" to apply your changes

Can I whitelist trusted websites? ▼

Yes, the extension provides flexible whitelisting options:

• Temporary Whitelist: Disable protection for a specific site for 1 hour

• Permanent Whitelist: Add trusted sites to your permanent whitelist

• Smart Whitelisting: The extension learns from your browsing patterns

To whitelist a site, click the extension icon while on the website and select "Add to Whitelist" or "Temporary Whitelist" from the popup menu.

You can manage your whitelist through the extension settings or the dashboard.

Is my browsing data private and secure? ▼

Yes, we take your privacy very seriously:

• No Personal Data Collection: We don't collect or store personal information

• Local Processing: Most security checks are performed locally on your device

• Encrypted Communications: All API communications use HTTPS encryption

• No Browsing History: We don't track or store your browsing history

• Anonymous Threat Queries: Threat detection queries are anonymized

• Open Source: The extension code is transparent and auditable

For more details, please review our Privacy Policy in the About section.

Troubleshooting

Solutions to common issues and problems

🔄

Extension Not Working

If the extension isn't working properly, try refreshing the page, restarting your browser, or disabling and re-enabling the extension in your browser settings.

⚠️

False Positive Alerts

If a trusted site is being blocked, add it to your whitelist. You can also report false positives to help improve our detection accuracy.

🐌

Slow Performance

Clear your browser cache, disable unnecessary extensions, or adjust the scanning sensitivity in the extension settings to improve performance.

📊

Statistics Not Updating

Refresh the extension popup or restart your browser. If the issue persists, check that the extension has proper permissions in your browser settings.

🔐

API Key Issues

Ensure your API keys are correctly configured in the settings. Some features require valid API keys from threat intelligence providers.

🌐

Website Compatibility

If a website isn't loading properly, try temporarily disabling the extension for that site or adding it to your whitelist.
