Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Data Collection and Usage

AI Threat Detector is designed with privacy-first principles. We collect minimal data necessary for security protection and never store personal information.

What We Collect:

  • Threat URLs: Only URLs identified as threats are sent to our security APIs for verification (anonymized)
  • Security Statistics: Anonymous blocking statistics to improve our threat detection algorithms
  • Error Reports: Optional crash reports to improve extension stability (no personal data included)

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Your browsing history or personal websites visited
  • Personal information, credentials, or form data
  • Search queries or typed content
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking data from websites

Data Security

All data transmissions use HTTPS encryption. Threat detection queries are anonymized and processed through secure APIs. No data is stored on our servers beyond temporary caching for performance.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with reputable security services:

  • Google Safe Browsing: For phishing and malware detection
  • VirusTotal: For comprehensive threat analysis
  • PhishTank: For phishing URL verification
  • URLScan.io: For website security analysis

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable any data collection features in extension settings
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your usage
  • Opt-out of anonymous statistics collection
  • Contact us with privacy concerns or questions

Contact Information

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at: privacy@aithreatdetector.com
